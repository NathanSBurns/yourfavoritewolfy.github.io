Radio Unifurse is a live podcast show airing Sunday evenings following The Funday PawPet Show. It's co-hosted by Sonicgott (Justin Reese) and DJ Blue (E. L. Rosengarten). The show features news and conventions topics, collaboration videos, guests, and videos. Dial-A-Song will be featured throughout the 2015 season.

Originally designed to be a four hour program, Radio Unifurse developed into a two hour show airing nearly every Sunday. After about a year, due to financial reasons, the program began to broadcast from Apple Valley, MN. A month later Kae Kiama joined as a co-host. In April of 2011, Radio Unifurse was once again relocated to Saint Paul, MN, and Blaine, MN.

DJ Blue joined the show during February 2012, and began hosting the broadcast from Charlotte, NC in 2013.
